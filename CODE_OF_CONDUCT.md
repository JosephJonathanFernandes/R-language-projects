# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and inclusive community. Be respectful, considerate, and collaborative in all interactions.

- Harassment and bullying are not tolerated.
- Assume good intent; disagree constructively.
- Respect different skill levels, backgrounds, and perspectives.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers via email or a private issue.

For a more detailed policy, see the Contributor Covenant at https://www.contributor-covenant.org/ and adapt as needed for your organization.
